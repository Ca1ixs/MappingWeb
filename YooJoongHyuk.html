Incarnation

Yoo Joonghyuk

Age: 28(3rd turn)
Race: Human/Superhuman
Modifier: Secretive Plotter

The main character of "Three Ways to Survive in a Ruined World" who became a companion of Kim Dokja. He has the power to regress whenever he's in a brink of death, allowing him to turn back time. He gained this power by signing a contract with the constellation "Oldest Dream".

In the original story "Three Ways to Survive in a Ruined World", he had regressed 2000+ times, which led him to witness the deaths of all his companions and comrades in various ways. However, after the novel story became a reality, his memories of his previous life in the world seem to have changed due to all the alterations made by Kim Dokja.
