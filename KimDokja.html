Incarnation/Constellation

Kim Dokja

Age: 28
Race: Human/Superhuman/Constellation
Modifier: "Demon King of Salvation"

After his favourite novel "Three Ways to Survive in a Ruined World" came to an end and became a reality. He then faces the challenge of surviving in his favourite novel with all the knowledge he got from the novel itself. He then has the goal to change all the fates of all his favourite characters inside the novel, striving to reach the end of the scenario with all the characters he loves so much.

He swore to himself that he will do anything in his power to avoid the deaths of the characters he loves so much. He became a constellation in order to achive a high power to maintain the safety of his companions and have establish connection in numerous constellations to gain upper hand in the events that awaits him.
