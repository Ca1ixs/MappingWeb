Age: ??

One of the constellation that love Kim Dokja story so much, Uriel is based on the biblical Uriel which is the Angel of death. She have the apperance of a girl with golden hair and black wings, she gave aid and notify Kim Dokja through out the journey. She's the first one to become remorseful after each Kim Dokja death's in the story.

Uriel is an archangel of Eden and is part of Absolute Good she have the modifier of "Demon-like Judge of Fire" which accurately describe her role in the story. She have the power of the Fire Sword that she use to terminate all evil that is in the side of Absolute Evil, she then later on have no choice but to use it on Kim Dokja in order to stop the destruction of seoul.
