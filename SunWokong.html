Constellation

Sun Wokong

Age: ??
Race: Demigod/Constellation
Modifier: Prisoner of the Golden Headband

One of the constellation that support Kim Dokja through out the journey to reach the end of the scenario. Sun Wokong is based on the chinese legend Sun Wokong, although in this story Sun Wokong is a constellation supporting Kim Dokja and helping him in every way he can. He have the modifier of "Prisoner of the Golden Headband".

Sun Wokong choose his sponsor in later on of the story in order to aid Kim Dokja even more. This lead to Kim Dokja having a powerful human companion with the power of the legendary Sun Wokong, with them being around they became a great help in Kim Dokja reaching the end.
