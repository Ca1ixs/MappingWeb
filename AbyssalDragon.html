One of the most loyal supporter of Kim Dokja Nebula, Abyssal Black Dragon power bring forth destruction and death to those who oppose him. His contracted to the incarnation Han Sooyoung, with the help of both Black Dragon and Han Sooyoung they are able to give a big help to Kim Dokja in reaching the end of the scenario.

In the original story "Three Ways to Survive in a Ruined World", his original incarnation is Kim Namwoon which is one of the original companion of Yoo Joonghyuk. After Kim Dokja kill's Kim Namwoon the original plot of the story started to shift.
