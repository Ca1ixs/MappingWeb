Incarnation

Lee Hyunsung

Age: 28
Race: Human/Superhuman
Modifier: Pure Steel Sword

A military soldier that coincidentally rode the same train cart with Kim Dokja and after being save from the first scenario he became the shield for Kim Dokja. Being contracted with Master of Steel he obtain the stigma that reside with Master of Steel, this allow him to become a shield or a sword itself.

In the original story "Three Ways to Survive in a Ruined World", he is one of the companion of Yoo Joonghyuk, he became the shield of their team. In Yoo Joonghyuk regression each time Lee Hyunsung die he always protect his comrade.
