Incarnation

Jung Heewon

Age: 27
Race: Human/Superhuman
Modifier: Destroyer of Evil

After being save by Kim Dokja, Jung Heewon became one of the loyal subordinate of Kim Dokja. She pledge to be Kim Dokja sword that will cut those who oppose him. She have contracted with the constellation Demon-Like Judge of Fire(Uriel) and obtain the power of the flame sword that can cut evil.

In the original "Three Ways to Survive the Apocalyptic World" Jung Heewon is bound to die, but being save by Kim Dokja alters the flow of the story. After being save she have contributed a huge role in advancing each scenario.
